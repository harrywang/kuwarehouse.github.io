I installed a Garage Heater so that I can work comfortably during winter.

Total Cost: ~ $260

- $165 (Comfort Zone CZ230ERBK Ceiling Mount Heater)
- $20 PVC conduit
- $40 8 gauge 8-2 wire
- $17 50 amp 2-pole breaker
- $15 tandem breaker 15 amp
